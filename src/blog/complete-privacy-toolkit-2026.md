# Your Complete Online Privacy Toolkit for 2026

## Tier 1: Essential — Do This Today

**AdShield Pro** — blocks ads, trackers, fingerprinting scripts, and malvertising in your browser. Free tier covers the essentials immediately.

**Password manager** — Bitwarden (free, open-source) or 1Password. Unique passwords on every site is non-negotiable for account security.

**Private search engine** — DuckDuckGo or Brave Search as your default. Takes 30 seconds to switch in browser settings.

## Tier 2: Significant Improvement

**Privacy-respecting DNS** — NextDNS or Cloudflare 1.1.1.1 with filtering. Free, configurable on your router to protect all home devices.

**2FA on all important accounts** — use an authenticator app (Aegis on Android, Raivo on iOS), not SMS.

**Firefox or Brave** as your primary browser — both have stronger privacy defaults than Chrome.

## Tier 3: Serious Privacy

**Trustworthy VPN** — Mullvad or ProtonVPN, approximately $5–$10/month.

**ProtonMail** for email — end-to-end encrypted, Swiss jurisdiction, no advertising business model.

**Signal** for messaging — end-to-end encrypted by default, no metadata collected.

## Tier 4: Maximum Protection

- Pi-hole on your home network for DNS-level blocking of every device
- Tor Browser for high-sensitivity browsing sessions
- Hardware security key (YubiKey) for critical accounts

You do not need to implement all four tiers. Even Tier 1 alone puts you dramatically ahead of the average internet user in terms of both privacy and security.
