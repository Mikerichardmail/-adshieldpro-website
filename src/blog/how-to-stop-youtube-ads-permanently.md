# How to Stop YouTube Ads Permanently in 2026

## Why YouTube Ads Are Getting Worse

YouTube has significantly increased its ad load in recent years. Unskippable ads appear before, during, and after videos. Some videos contain mid-roll breaks every 2–3 minutes. YouTube has also actively worked to detect and circumvent ad blockers — which is why network-level blocking matters.

## What AdShield Pro Blocks on YouTube

- **Pre-roll ads** — 5–30 second ads before videos start
- **Unskippable ads** — ads where the skip button never appears
- **Mid-roll ads** — interruptions during longer videos
- **Bumper ads** — 6-second non-skippable ads
- **Display ads** — banner ads overlaying the video player
- **Overlay ads** — semi-transparent ads at the bottom of the video

## How the Blocking Works

AdShield Pro intercepts requests to YouTube's ad-serving infrastructure (`googleadservices.com`, `doubleclick.net`) before they are fulfilled. Because the ad request is blocked at the network level, YouTube's player receives no ad content to display — rather than trying to hide ad elements after they load. This approach is more resistant to YouTube's anti-adblock detection.

## Staying Current

YouTube regularly updates its ad delivery to defeat blockers. If an ad slips through:

1. Check AdShield Pro is on the latest version
2. Force a filter update: shield icon → Settings → Filter Lists → Update All
3. Report persistent issues via the [support page](https://adshieldpro.org/support)
