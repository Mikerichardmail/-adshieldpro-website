# Your 6-Month Privacy Checkup: 12 Essential Tasks

## Why Regular Maintenance Matters

Tracking technology evolves constantly. Filter lists need updates, extension versions change, and services you trusted may be acquired by companies with different data practices. A regular privacy checkup takes 20–30 minutes and keeps your protections current.

## Browser and Extensions

- Update AdShield Pro and force a filter list refresh (Shield icon → Settings → Filter Lists → Update All)
- Audit all installed extensions — remove anything you no longer use
- Review browser permissions for all sites (Settings → Site Settings)
- Check that HTTPS-Only mode is still enabled

## Accounts and Passwords

- Run HaveIBeenPwned.com on all your email addresses
- Rotate passwords on high-value accounts (email, banking, cloud storage)
- Enable 2FA on any account that does not already have it
- Review active sessions on important accounts and revoke old ones

## Social Media

- Review ad preference settings on Google, Facebook, and TikTok — opt out of interest-based advertising
- Check which third-party apps have access to your social accounts and revoke unused ones
- Review your data download to see what each platform holds about you

## Devices

- Update your router's firmware (most people never do this)
- Review app permissions on your phone — location, microphone, and contacts in particular
- Check smart TV privacy settings and disable ACR if it has been re-enabled by a firmware update
- Verify DNS-over-HTTPS is still configured on your router

## Services

- Review subscriptions for services you no longer use (less data stored = less breach exposure)
- Check whether any services you use have had a breach since your last checkup
