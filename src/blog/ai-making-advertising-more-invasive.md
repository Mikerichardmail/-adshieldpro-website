# How AI Is Making Online Advertising More Invasive Than Ever

## Predictive Behavioural Targeting

Modern advertising platforms use machine learning models trained on billions of behavioural signals to predict not just your current interests but your future purchasing intent, emotional state, and vulnerability to specific persuasion techniques.

When you are sleep-deprived (indicated by late-night browsing patterns), experiencing relationship stress (indicated by search queries), or financially pressured (indicated by financial comparison site visits), these signals are detected and used to calibrate ad messaging for maximum effectiveness.

## The Lookalike Audience Problem

Even if you have never directly shared data with an advertiser, your lookalike audience profile — inferred from people with similar browsing patterns, demographics, and behaviours — is used to target you. You are profiled by proxy, without any direct data collection from you.

## Generative Ad Creative

AI is now generating personalised ad creative in real time — not just selecting which ad to show, but generating ad copy and imagery tailored specifically to your inferred preferences, emotional state, and cultural context. Every ad you see can be unique to you.

## What Still Works Against AI Advertising

AI-generated creative is still delivered through the same ad networks and CDNs.
