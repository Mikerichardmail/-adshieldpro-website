# Google's $225 Billion Advertising Business: How It Actually Works

## The Scale of Google's Advertising Business

Google's advertising revenue for 2025 exceeded $225 billion — the largest advertising operation in history. This business is built on the most comprehensive consumer profiling operation ever created, and it follows you across the web regardless of whether you use Google products.

## The Three Revenue Streams

**Google Search Ads:** Advertisers bid in a real-time auction on every search you perform, using your query, location, search history, and Google account profile to determine which ads you see.

**YouTube Advertising:** Pre-roll, mid-roll, and display ads targeted using your YouTube watch history, Google account, and cross-site browsing data.

**Google Display Network:** The component that follows you across the web. Any website using Google Ad Manager, AdSense, or DoubleClick participates. When you visit, Google's servers look up your profile and serve the highest-bidding ad in real time.

## The Tracking Infrastructure

- **Google Analytics** is present on ~86% of the top million websites, feeding your page views to Google
- **Google Tag Manager** loads additional tracking scripts on millions of publisher sites
- **DoubleClick / Google Ad Manager** tracks you across every participating publisher via cookie and fingerprint
- **Google Sign-In buttons** report your visit when they load — even if you do not click them
