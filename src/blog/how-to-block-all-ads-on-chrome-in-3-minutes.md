# How to Block All Ads on Chrome in 3 Minutes

## The Fastest Path to Ad-Free Browsing

Chrome is the world's most popular browser — and it is loaded with ads by default. This guide gets you to a completely ad-free, tracker-free experience in under three minutes.

## Step 1 — Install AdShield Pro

Visit the AdShield Pro Chrome Web Store listing and click **Add to Chrome**. The extension is under 200 KB and installs in seconds.

## Step 2 — Pin It to Your Toolbar

Click the puzzle-piece icon in Chrome's toolbar, find AdShield Pro, and click the pin icon. The shield icon will now appear on every tab showing blocked element counts.

## Step 3 — Verify It's Working

Visit any major news website. The page should load noticeably faster and be free of banner ads, pop-ups, and sidebar advertisements.

## Optional: Whitelist Sites You Want to Support

1. Navigate to that site
2. Click the AdShield Pro shield icon
3. Toggle **"Block on this site"** to off

Your whitelist is stored entirely locally — no account, no login required.

## What Changes Immediately

- Banner ads disappear from news and shopping sites
- Pre-roll ads on YouTube are blocked before they load
- Pop-ups and overlays are removed automatically
- Page load times drop significantly (often 50–70% faster)
- Data usage per page falls on metered connections
