# The 5 Best Privacy-Focused Search Engines in 2026

## Why Search Privacy Matters

Your search history reveals health concerns, relationship problems, financial worries, and political views. Google retains your search history indefinitely and cross-references it with Gmail, YouTube history, and location data.

## 1. DuckDuckGo

No tracking, no personalisation, no query logging. Results powered by Bing with DuckDuckGo-specific sources. Excellent for most everyday queries. Free.

## 2. Brave Search

Independent search index — does not proxy Google or Bing results. Completely private with a Goggles feature for custom ranking. Free.

## 3. Startpage

Proxies Google results without sending your identity to Google. Google-quality results with complete privacy. Anonymous View opens results through a proxy. Free.

## 4. Kagi

Paid subscription ($5–14/month), zero advertising, genuinely excellent results. The subscription model means no incentive to track you. Best results quality among private search engines.

## 5. SearXNG

Open-source, self-hostable metasearch engine. Run your own instance for zero third-party dependency. Ideal for technically minded users.

## The Recommendation

Switch to **DuckDuckGo** as your default immediately — takes 30 seconds in browser settings. For results quality, consider **Kagi**. Combine any of these with AdShield Pro to block the tracking infrastructure that follows you even after switching search engines.
