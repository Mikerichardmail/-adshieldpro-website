FAQ

Clear answers about privacy, permissions, and upgrading.

What permissions does it need?

AdShield Pro uses Chrome permissions like declarativeNetRequest (blocking), storage (local settings), and scripting (cleaning page elements). These are used for functionality, not tracking.

Does it collect any data?

No personal data, no analytics, and no tracking. Settings are stored locally on your device. See Privacy Policy.

How do I uninstall it?

Chrome → Extensions → Manage Extensions → Remove.
