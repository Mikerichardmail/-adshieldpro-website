Troubleshooting

Extension not showing in the toolbar?
Chrome menu → Extensions → Pin AdShield Pro.
Ads still visible on a site?
Reload the page, check if the site is allowlisted, and confirm AdShield Pro is enabled for that tab.
A site breaks after blocking?
Temporarily disable on that site or add it to your allowlist, then refresh.
Pro key not activating?
Double-check for extra spaces, confirm you’re using the latest extension version, and try verifying again. If it still fails, email support with your Gumroad receipt/order details.

FAQ

How do I uninstall?
Chrome → Extensions → Manage Extensions → Remove.
Does AdShield Pro collect any data?
No personal data, no tracking, and no analytics. Settings are stored locally on your device. See Privacy Policy.
How do I upgrade later?
You can upgrade any time from the Pricing page. Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription).
