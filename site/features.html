Features

AdShield Pro focuses on effective blocking, privacy protection, and simple controls. Everything runs locally on your device.

Ad & Tracker Blocking

  • Blocks ads and known tracking endpoints using rule-based filtering.
  • Reduces intrusive page elements that interrupt reading and scrolling.
  • Works automatically after installation with sensible defaults.
  • Supports allowlisting trusted sites when you want to support publishers.

Privacy Protection

  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking scripts.
  • No personal data collection and no browsing history collection.
  • Offline-first approach: core blocking does not rely on remote services.
  • Settings are stored locally using Chrome storage.

Performance & Data Saver

  • Helps pages load faster by blocking unnecessary requests.
  • Reduces clutter to keep scrolling and interaction smoother.
  • Designed to be lightweight and avoid slowing down your browser.
  • Optional Pro controls for more advanced performance tuning.

Control & Customization

  • Quick enable/disable and per-site controls.
  • Allowlist websites you trust.
  • Cosmetic filtering controls (Pro) for cleaner layouts.
  • Local-only settings so your preferences remain on your device.
